ELON MUSK

Elon Reeve Musk was born June 28th, 1971 in Pretoria, South Africa. At age 10, Elon taught himself how to code and when he was 12, sold a video game, he named Blastar, to a magazine called PC and Office Technology, for $500. Now Elon is the Founder, CEO, Lead Designer of SpaceX CEO, Product Architect of Tesla, Inc., Co-founder, CEO of Neuralink, Founder of The Boring Company, Co-founder of Zip2, Founder of X.com (now PayPal), Co-founder of OpenAI, and Chairman of SolarCity.


Entrepreneur Elon Musk has achieved global fame as the chief executive officer (CEO) of electric automobile maker Tesla Inc. (TSLA) and the private space company SpaceX. Musk was an early investor in several tech companies, and in October 2022, he completed a deal to take Twitter Inc. private. His success and personal style have given rise to comparisons to other colorful tycoons from U.S. history, including Steve Jobs, Howard Hughes, and Henry Ford. He was named the richest person in the world with an estimated net worth of $212 billion as of October 2022. 1 Musk first achieved that distinction in 2021, surpassing Amazon.com Inc. (AMZN) founder Jeff Bezos.
